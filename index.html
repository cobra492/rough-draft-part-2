Nice to meet you

My name is Kyle and I am a fourth year English and professional writing student at York University. I am an aspiring author and amateur web author. My skills lie more in the making social media contents such as TikTok and short videos for YouTube, with having created several different vlogs, including a review on Wattpad, as well as an example for a new mechanic for tiktok live streams. My relationship with computers has started out shaky, but after some hard work, determination, and a few casualties, I have developed a greater understanding of computers, but still have a lot to learn.

I am interested in video editing and social media content, as well as vlogging about some of my favourite things. In my head I have several different vlogs, but in reality, the only thing that I post on social media is funny videos that I find online, with some of them doing very well. I would love to vlog for either fashion, or gaming companies as well as possibly do anything in PR that involves digital authoring.

I first got into digital authoring writing books of my favourite characters online and posting them, but the evidence for all that is all burned, I made sure of that. We all have a past no one wants to investigate

.

Despite my past with vlogging and computers, I feel like I am growing as a digital author and hoping to hone my skills.
